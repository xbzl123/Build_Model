Snapshots

From the main menu, tapto go to the Snapshots screen.

Delete Device Snapshots

From the Snapshots screen, swipe left on a device until the Delete button appears. Tap Delete to delete all snapshots from this device.

Send Snapshots

To email images, follow the instructions below:
  1. From the Snapshots screen, select a device to go to preview images.
  2. Tap to select an snapshot, or tap Edit to select multiple snapshots. Tapto send the selected image(s).
  3. Tap to select your email app, then enter the email address you would like to send the snapshot(s) to. Tap Send to complete.

Delete Snapshots

To remove multiple images or recording files, follow the steps below:

  1. From the Snapshots screen, select a device to go to preview images.
  2. Tap Option, then tap to select images you would like to remove.
  3. Tap Delete to remove the selected images, or tap Clear to delete all images.

Image Manager

Instruction:
  1. From the Snapshots screen, tap an image to enlarge it to full screen. Tap to display images automatically. Tap to remove images.
  2. You can pinch to zoom in/out images, double-tap to enlarge images, or swipe left/right to view the previous/next image.
